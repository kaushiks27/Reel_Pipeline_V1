# Step 6: Video Prompts — All Scenes (Veo 3.1 + Lip-Sync)

> **Target tool**: Google Veo 3.1 (native lip-sync generation)
> **Source**: Coursework (`33_KlingCameraPDF`, `36_Kling_3.0_UPDATE`, `41_Consistent_Character_Voices_in_Veo_3.1`)
> **Key rules applied**:
> 1. Start prompt with camera angle (per Kling toolkit — also works with Veo)
> 2. Structure: Shot → Details → Camera → Mood (per Kling 3.0 examples)
> 3. Voice blueprint copy-pasted verbatim (per Veo 3.1 consistency)
> 4. `...` pauses for natural rhythm (per Veo voice lesson)
> 5. Dialogue kept short — max 10 words per clip (per Veo: "upload too much dialogue → speaks super fast")
> 6. Subtle camera movement only — concept-animated style, not action cinema
> 7. **Lip-sync**: Character speaks dialogue ON CAMERA — Veo 3.1 generates lip movement
> 8. Dialogue written as what the character SAYS, not narration description

## Prompt Design Rationale

| Decision | Coursework Source | Why |
|----------|-------------------|-----|
| **Start with camera angle** | `33_KlingCameraPDF` — "IMPORTANT: Always start your prompt with the camera angle" | Kling recognizes camera movement immediately |
| **Shot/Details/Camera/Mood structure** | `36_Kling_3.0_UPDATE` — every example follows this pattern | Proven structure for Kling 3.0 |
| **"Subtle animation" not dramatic motion** | Concept-animated style, not photorealistic action | Prevents AI from adding excessive movement that breaks the art style |
| **Voice blueprint verbatim** | `41_Veo_3.1` — "define the voice once... copy paste into every prompt" | Ensures voice consistency across all clips |
| **`...` pauses in dialogue** | `41_Veo_3.1` — "adds punctuation... lets AI know to pause naturally" | Natural rhythm, not robotic reading |
| **Short dialogue lines** | `41_Veo_3.1` — "8 seconds... speak super fast" | Each clip has ≤10 words of dialogue |
| **Slow dolly in for anchors** | `33_KlingCameraPDF` #2 — "slow dolly in for emotional intensity" | Builds intimacy without distracting movement |
| **Slightly low angle for B1** | Script spec: "(child's perspective)" + `33_KlingCameraPDF` #10 | Shows mother meeting child at their level |
| **Slow pan for B2** | `33_KlingCameraPDF` #9 — "slow pan to reveal" | Reveals the connection between parent and child |
| **Slow crane up for B3** | `33_KlingCameraPDF` #7 — "crane up for transcendence" | Creates a "sending into the world" feeling |
