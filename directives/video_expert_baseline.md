## Core Knowledge Summary

### 1. Prompting Fundamentals (6-Step Method)
1. **Purpose** — what + where + how long
2. **Role** — who the AI acts as (e.g., "world-class film director")
3. **Structure/Format** — exact output shape
4. **Style/Tone** — voice and vibe
5. **Details/Constraints** — rules, limits, must-haves
6. **Example + Action** — weak → upgraded prompt

### 2. Image Creation (Nanobanana PRO / Midjourney / Seedream)
- **Multi-turn editing** — iterative refinement (rough → final)
- **Multi-image fusion** — composite actor into world
- **Pose control** — stick figure reference method
- **Multiple objects trick** — label everything in Canva, upload as single board
- **Talk like your crew, not like a prompt engineer**

### 3. Avatar Realism (8 Prompt Modifier Categories)
1. Core photorealism triggers (`photorealistic`, `ultra-realistic`, `natural imperfections`)
2. Camera & lens (`35mm`, `50mm`, `85mm`, `shallow depth of field`, `natural bokeh`)
3. Lighting (`natural light`, `golden hour`, `practical lighting` — avoid fantasy lighting)
4. Texture & detail (`visible pores`, `fabric grain`, `slight imperfections`)
5. Color & tone (`natural color grading`, `muted tones`, `cinematic color balance`)
6. Composition (`rule of thirds`, `candid moment`, `foreground/background separation`)
7. Grain & quality (`subtle film grain`, `sharp focus`, `clean but not overly polished`)
8. Negative words (`no cartoon`, `no CGI`, `no plastic skin`, `no unrealistic lighting`)

### 4. Video Generation
- **Kling 3.0/Omni** — multimodal input, element consistency, storyboarding (3-15s), native audio, video reference for camera/actions, style transfer
- **Veo 3.1** — voice blueprint method (define once, copy-paste exact description), keep dialogue short, use punctuation for natural pauses, lock accent for consistency
- **Higgsfield** — all-in-one platform (Kling + Nanobanana PRO)

### 5. Camera Movement Toolkit (30 + 6 + Combos)
**Always start prompt with camera angle.** Key movements: handheld, slow dolly in/out, crane up/down, over-shoulder, 360° orbit, tracking behind, POV camera, push-in zoom, environmental reveal, through-object transitions. Combo examples: push-in + tilt up, crane down + orbit, first-person + handheld + sudden fall.

### 6. Voice Consistency (Veo 3.1)
- Define voice once: accent + tone + pacing + personality
- Copy-paste exact description into every prompt
- Keep dialogue SHORT (8-second limit per clip)
- Use `...` and punctuation for natural pauses
- Choose accent that matches character (limits voice pool → more consistency)
- **16 tone presets**: conversational, confident, energetic, casual, storytelling, calm, persuasive, professional, dramatic, curious, friendly, sarcastic, authority, reflective, conversational story, fast social media
- **Fallback**: ElevenLabs Voice Changer for post-hoc consistency

### 7. The 8-Step Video Workflow
1. ChatGPT — Concept & Planning
2. ChatGPT — Scriptwriting & Voice Planning
3. ChatGPT / Midjourney / Seedream / Nanobanana PRO — Image Creation
4. Canva — Image Editing & Lighting Touch-up
5. Gigapixel / Midjourney upscale / Bigjpg / Nanobanana PRO — Upscaling
6. Kling / Veo 3 / Higgsfield — AI Video Generation
7. ElevenLabs & Pixabay — Sound Effects & Voiceover
8. CapCut — Final Editing

### 8. Sound Design (ElevenLabs)
- 100 example prompts across: ancient/historical, war, time travel, fantasy, comedy, cinematic
- Pattern: descriptive scene audio + environmental context + emotional modifier
