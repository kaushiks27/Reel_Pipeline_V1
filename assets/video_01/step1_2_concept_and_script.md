## Vibe Brainstorm

**Overall feel**: This video should feel like a wise grandmother sitting you down and saying "it's okay, anak — here's what to do." Not lecturing, not panicking about grades. The emotional truth is: your child already feels bad. What they need from you is safety, not anger.

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| **Emotional register** | Empathetic, never critical. The viewer should think "yes, I should respond this way" not "I'm a bad parent" |
| **Pacing feel** | Gentle rhythm, each rule lands, pauses, then the next flows in |
| **Color mood** | Golden warmth (lamplight, afternoon sun) — comforting, like a safe space |
| **Visual metaphor** | Every B-roll shows physical closeness (arm around shoulder, sitting on floor together, high-five) — the message is: be WITH your child, not against them |
| **Sound feeling** | Warm, steady, grandmother's voice of wisdom |
| **What it should NOT feel like** | Scolding, guilt-tripping, academic pressure, corporate advice |
