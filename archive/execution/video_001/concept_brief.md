### My Recommendation: **Topic 1 — "3 Simple Habits That Raise Respectful Kids"**

**Why this is the best first video:**
- **"Respect"** is universally understood and deeply valued in Filipino culture — it's the topic that will resonate with the *widest* audience on Facebook
- As the first video, it establishes Lola as a trusted elder sharing practical habits (not lecturing)
- The 3 habits naturally produce positive, happy B-roll (kids greeting elders, saying "po," helping at home)
- Zero NSFW risk — every scene is inherently wholesome
- Creates a pattern viewers can expect: "Lola shares 3 simple habits" → builds series anticipation

| Dimension | Profile |
|-----------|---------|

| **Gender** | 70% female (mothers), 30% male (fathers, grandparents) |
| **Location** | Philippines — Metro Manila, Cebu, Davao, Visayas provinces |
| **Cultural context** | Filipino family values: respect for elders, close extended family, bayanihan, faith-based moral framework |
| **Pain points** | "Am I raising my child right?" / "My child is becoming disrespectful" / "Screen time is replacing family time" / "I don't want to be too strict but I don't want to be too soft" |
| **Desires** | Raise a respectful, confident, happy child. Be a "good parent" that others admire. Pass on Filipino values to the next generation. |
