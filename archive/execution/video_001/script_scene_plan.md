## 2.1 — Complete Voiceover Script

**Word count: 98 words | Estimated duration: ~38 seconds at natural pace**

```
[HOOK]
Want your child to grow up respectful? Start with these three.

[HABIT 1]
Rule one... greet people politely.
When your child sees you do "mano po" to your elders...
they learn respect... without you saying a word.

[HABIT 2]
Two... say "thank you" at the dinner table.
A small word... repeated every day...
becomes a lifetime habit.

[HABIT 3]
Three... let them help around the house.
When they help... they feel responsible.
And a responsible child... is a respectful child.

[CTA]
Try just one tonight... and watch what happens.
Follow for more.
```

**Pause markers (`...`) placed per Lesson 41:** "Use punctuation for natural pauses. Ellipses create breathing room that sounds human."
