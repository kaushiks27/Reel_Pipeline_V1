What Is Higgsfield Relight?
Higgsfield Relight is a post-lighting system. Meaning you can instantly change the lighting on a photo without any hassle, even after it's already been taken / generated.

Relight allows you to:

Add new light sources

Change direction, intensity, and mood

Simulate cinematic lighting setups

Fix flat, dull, or poorly lit visuals

Match lighting across multiple shots for consistency

Think of it like having a virtual film lighting crew that is working for you 🚀

How Higgsfield Relight Works
You can define:

Light direction (side, top, back, under)

All you need to do is drag where you want the lighting to come from

Light type (soft, hard) 

Hard light is strong and direct. It creates sharp, dark shadows and high contrast, which is similar to the sun at midday, making textures and details stand out and giving a dramatic look

Soft light is spread out and gentle. It wraps around the subject, creates smooth shadows, like the light on a cloudy day—resulting in a more natural and flattering look



3. Color temperature and brightness 

(warm, neutral, cold) - these will influence the mood of your image (you can select any colour and how bright you want the light to look).
