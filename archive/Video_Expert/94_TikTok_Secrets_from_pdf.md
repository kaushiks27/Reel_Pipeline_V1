The secrets of TikTok nobody tells you     
Things you should do:    
1. Find your preferred posting time and then post at the same time every day. This way you 
can track metrics without posting time affecting them. 
2. Comment bait. Add something annoying or random, or something that is completely out 
of place, into your video. People will point it out, increasing the number of comments and 
pushing you further into the algorithm. 
3. Add a follower goal in your bio. For example, 6500/7000  . People love to feel like they 
are making a difference, so they’ll follow you. 
4. Post 3-5 times a day MAXIMUM. 
5. If you want a specific viewership demographic, e.g USA, you can make content focused 
around USA and TikTok will naturally push it to viewers there. Use hashtags like #USA 
#Newyork 
6. Track your metrics, watch time is the most crucial factor, if you see people are scrolling 
away at 2 seconds, change your next video. 
7. Make friends on the platform and message each other to comment on each other's post 
every time you post. If you get 10 people who do this, that is 10 comments on your video 
instantly. 
8. Every time you post, screenshot the video and post it to your story too. 
9. Use maximum 4 hashtags, anymore and the algorithm gets confused. 
10. Add text to your videos, if people are reading, that means they are still watching. 
11. Engage with videos similar to your niche. Like, comment and watch videos for at least 10-
15 minutes a day. This way TikTok knows the sort of audience to push your content to. 
12. Make your page look aesthetically pleasing by having similar thumbnails for each video. 
13. Use momentum. If one of your videos is doing well, do not wait a few days to post again. 
Post frequently, this way the viewers on your other video will see your latest content too 
and it is more likely to go viral. 
14. Have a clear and precise bio, with emojis in it.  
15. Bookmark and save video ideas. If you run out of ideas, you can refer back to your 
bookmarks and copy someone's video. 
16. Reply to every single comment on your videos. Even better, ask people a question in your 
reply. This will drive more engagement. 
17. 
Make the first clip of your video no longer than 3 seconds. People want to see 
engaging content, if they have to wait 5 seconds for a scene change, they are probably 
going to scroll.

18. Make social media accounts on every platform. YouTube/insta/TikTok. If you have made 
the video already (the hard part), it is easy to spend a few extra minutes posting it 
EVERYWHERE. This way you don’t limit yourself to one platform and can 2/3x your reach. 
19. Follow trends. If you see someone in your niche posted a video YESTERDAY and it has 
2million views. Recreate that exact video, with a few changes, and post it ASAP. Do not 
try to re-invent the wheel, just post content that you see is working for other people. 
 
Things you should not do    
1. Log into someone elses account on your phone, the change of location will IP ban you 
immediately 
2. Upload to a new account straight away – you need to warm the account up by watching 
content in your niche for at least 7 days. You can check to see if your account is warmed 
up by following someone, if you return to your account page and your number of 
followers has increased, you are good to go. Aswell as this make sure you upload a 
random video before posting content, if you get 0 views, your account is not ready yet. 
3. NEVER delete videos, just private them. TikTok hates when videos are deleted. 
4. You should not continue to use your TikTok account normally. If you want to watch 
videos, go on another account. Never watch videos outside of your niche. 
5. Never post more than once within an hour. If you have posted, wait at least 1-2 hours 
before posting again. You will notice a decrease in views if you post too frequently. 
6. DO NOT use the promote video feature. TikTok will see that you are happy to pay for 
views and limit the reach of any videos you do not promote in the future. 
7. Do not use copyrighted sounds if your video is over 1 minute long. You can get away with 
it if it is shorter but never go over 1 minute. Your video will be flagged as unoriginal. 
8. If you live, for example, in the UK, and you have 95% UK audience viewership, do NOT 
take your phone with you when you go on holiday. If you say go to Thailand, and log onto 
TikTok, TikTok now thinks you are from Thailand so will push your videos to the Thai 
community. You may never recover from this. 
9. Do not over-reply with emojis only - while replies are good, TikTok favors replies with full 
sentences or questions—these drive more interaction than “  ” or “ ”. 
10. TikTok is extremely strict, so do not include any objects in your video that could get it 
removed. For example, having cigars or cigarettes on a table can have your video 
removed, or showing a gun. 
11. Never post content that does not match your niche. Not even one video.

Russian), this makes TikTok think that you have a Russian fanbase and pushes your video 
to more of this viewership.

immediately downgrade the quality. Upload TikTok's in 1080p 30fps. Trust me it will look
