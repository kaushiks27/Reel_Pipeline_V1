# 👾 Levels Explained

---

🚀 **In our community, levels = rewards for being an active member!** The more you participate, the more rewards you unlock!

> **(THERE IS NO CONTENT LOCKED BEHIND ENGAGEMENT, WE DO NOT FORCE YOU TO ENGAGE!)**

## 🎯 HOW IT WORKS

- Every **post**, **comment**, and **like** in the community feed earns you **XP**.
- **XP = Levels.**
- **Levels = Access to cool titles and rewards!**

> **AT EACH LEVEL YOU WILL UNLOCK A NEW TITLE FOR YOUR PROFILE!**
