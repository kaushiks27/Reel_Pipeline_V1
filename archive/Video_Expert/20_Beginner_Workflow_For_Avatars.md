# Beginner Workflow For Avatars

The best image generator for AI Influencers/Avatars (for maximum quality and consistency) is **Nanobanana PRO**.

> After watching the lessons above:

---

## Step 1 — Start With A Rough Character Idea

Before generating anything, start with a simple concept for your avatar.

It doesn't need to be complicated — just decide the general type of character you want to create.

**Examples:**

- 23-year-old fitness influencer
- Luxury lifestyle influencer
- Travel vlogger
- Gamer girl streamer

You're simply deciding the **identity and vibe** of the character.

---

## Step 2 — Ask ChatGPT To Help Build The Prompt

Now take your rough idea and ask ChatGPT to turn it into a detailed NanoBanana PRO prompt.

**Example message you could send:**

> *Turn this idea into a detailed NanoBanana Pro prompt for generating a realistic AI influencer.*
>
> *Idea: A 24 year old lifestyle influencer who posts coffee shop and travel content.*

ChatGPT will then generate a much more detailed prompt.

---

## Step 3 — Open NanoBanana Pro

NanoBanana Pro can be accessed through:

- **Higgsfield AI**
- **Gemini**

Once inside:

1. Navigate to the **Image Generation** tool
2. Select **NanoBanana Pro** as the model
3. Make sure **image generation mode** is active

You should now see the main interface with:

- Prompt input box
- Image upload area
- Generation settings
- Generate button

---

## Step 4 — Paste Your Prompt

Now paste your prompt into the prompt box.

For the first generation:

- **Leave the reference image section empty**
- **Only use the prompt**

Then click **Generate**.

NanoBanana PRO will produce images for you.

> **Your goal here is to find a face and look that you like.**

---

## Step 5 — Download The Best Images

After the generation finishes:

1. Review all the outputs
2. Pick the **most realistic image** and the main one you want to stick with
3. **Download it**

This will become your **reference image** for your influencer.

---

## Step 6 — Upload Character References

Now we **lock the character identity**.

In Higgsfield or Gemini you can upload images.

**Workflow:**

1. Click **Upload Image / Reference**
2. Select the images you downloaded
3. Upload the image

These reference images tell NanoBanana PRO:

> **"Keep generating this same person."**

**This is what creates character consistency.**

---

## Step 7 — Generate New Content With The Avatar

Now you can generate new scenes using the same character.

Simply:

1. Keep the reference images uploaded
2. Enter a **new prompt** describing the scene
3. Click **Generate**

NanoBanana PRO will now create images while **keeping the same face**.

> We will go into more detail later in the course.

You can generate things like:

- Gym photos
- Travel photos
- Car selfies
- Lifestyle images
- Brand content

All with the **same AI influencer**.

---

## Step 8 — Build A Content Library

Once the avatar looks consistent, start generating multiple scenarios.

**Examples:**

- Apartment photos
- Coffee shop photos
- Mirror selfies
- Gym images
- Travel photos
- Brand Content

Download the best outputs and organise them.

**Example folder structure:**

```
AI Influencer/
├── Portraits/
├── Selfies/
├── Lifestyle/
├── Gym/
├── Travel/
└── Brand Content/
```

Now you have a **content library** for your avatar.

---

## Step 9 — Turn Images Into Video

Once you have strong images, you can animate them.

**Typical workflow:**

1. Export the avatar image
2. Upload it to **Kling**, **Veo**, or **Higgsfield** video tools
3. Add motion or lip sync

This allows you to create:

- Talking AI influencers
- Moving selfie videos
- UGC ads
- TikTok style content

> **PLEASE SEE PHASE 3 FOR VIDEO GENERATION**

---

## Final Workflow Summary

The entire process looks like this:

1. Create a rough avatar idea
2. Use ChatGPT to help you build the prompt
3. Open NanoBanana Pro
4. Paste the prompt
5. Generate the character
6. Download the best images
7. Upload them as references
8. Generate new scenes with the same avatar
9. Build a content library
10. Turn images into video

> Once your avatar exists, you can generate **unlimited content** with the same digital person.
