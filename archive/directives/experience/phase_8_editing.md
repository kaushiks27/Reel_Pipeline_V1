# Experience — Phase 8 Editing (CapCut)

## Reverse Prompting Q&A (March 19, 2026)

1. **Voiceover track:** N/A — Veo generated voice+lip-sync on all anchor clips. No separate voiceover track. → **Audio stays on video clips.**
2. **Lip-sync protection:** Never unlink audio from video on anchor clips. BG music and SFX on separate tracks below. → **Critical rule.**
3. **Audio ducking:** Lower music to 8-10% under anchor clips, raise to 20-25% under B-roll. → **Confirmed.**
4. **Transitions:** Quick dissolves (0.2-0.3s) at anchor↔B-roll boundaries, hard cuts elsewhere. → **Confirmed.**
5. **Captions:** CapCut auto-captions on anchor clips, manually verify against script. → **Confirmed.**

## Key Learning

- Veo 3.1 lip-sync success means Phase 8 has only 2 audio tracks (music + SFX) instead of 3 (+ voiceover). Simpler timeline.
- Audio ducking is the main technique for balancing Veo's voice against background music.
