# Experience — Phase 4 Image Editing

## Learnings (March 19, 2026)

1. **Animated style produces much cleaner images than photorealistic** — only 1 out of 12 images needed a definite edit (speech bubble in Scene 6). No AI artifacts (extra fingers, warped geometry, text glitches) were found in any image. This is a major advantage of the animated Pixar-adjacent style over photorealistic approaches.
2. **Speech bubbles get baked into images** — when the prompt mentions dialogue (e.g., "says Salamat, Nanay"), the AI may render a speech bubble. Learning for future prompts: describe the ACTION of speaking, not the literal words, to avoid baked-in text. E.g., "child speaks to mother with grateful expression" instead of "child says Salamat, Nanay."
3. **Nanobanana PRO output needs no major color/brightness corrections** — the animated style with warm golden palette prompts produces consistently warm images that match each other well. Phase 4 editing is minimal for animated content.
